NL: How many accounts are in district 5?
SQL: SELECT count(*) FROM account WHERE district_id = 5
---
NL: List all cards issued after 1995.
SQL: SELECT * FROM card WHERE issued > '1995-01-01'
---
NL: How many regions are there?
SQL: SELECT count(*) FROM regions
---
NL: Find accounts opened on 1993-01-01.
SQL: SELECT * FROM account WHERE date = '1993-01-01'
---
NL: Count cards of type junior.
SQL: SELECT count(*) FROM card WHERE type = 'junior'
---
NL: Get district name for district_id 10.
SQL: SELECT A2 FROM district WHERE district_id = 10
---
NL: Find cards issued to disp_id 100.
SQL: SELECT * FROM card WHERE disp_id = 100
---
NL: How many accounts have frequency POPLATEK PO OBRATU?
SQL: SELECT count(*) FROM account WHERE frequency = 'POPLATEK PO OBRATU'
---
NL: List regions with region_id less than 5.
SQL: SELECT * FROM regions WHERE region_id < 5
---
NL: Find accounts in Prague.
SQL: SELECT T1.* FROM account T1 JOIN district T2 ON T1.district_id = T2.district_id WHERE T2.A3 = 'Prague'
